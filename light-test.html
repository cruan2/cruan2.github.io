02 // ABOUT

EXPERIENCE & SKILLS.

I'm a QA Engineer II at Wynshop (acquired by Instacart), specializing in quality engineering and test automation. I've modernized release processes toward CI/CD-driven pipelines with GitHub Actions and Jenkins, designed scalable API automation frameworks — including three production-grade suites built on Serenity BDD — and built reporting and observability workflows with InfluxDB and Grafana to surface flaky tests, blocked deployments, and release bottlenecks.

Before that: web QA at Ritual, where I led testing for online ordering and prevented critical payment defects from reaching production, and SDET at CaseWare International. I hold a Computer Science degree from the University of Toronto. Outside of work I build games — which keeps me sharp on the other side of the bug tracker.
