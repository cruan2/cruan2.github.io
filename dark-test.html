02 // ABOUT

RUNNER PROFILE.

By day I'm a QA Engineer II at Wynshop (acquired by Instacart), where I've spent 6+ years building the machinery that catches bugs before customers do — API-first automation frameworks, CI/CD pipelines on GitHub Actions and Jenkins, and Grafana dashboards that tell us exactly which test is lying. I own 1,000+ automated tests — over half my org's coverage — and I've load-tested services past 20,000 concurrent API calls just to watch them sweat.

By night, all that professional paranoia gets pointed at my own game projects — which is humbling, because now the bugs are mine. I studied Computer Science at the University of Toronto, and my whole career has been finding the fastest route to “wait, why does it do that?” Turns out that's also most of game development.
